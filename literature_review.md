# Literature Review

## Industrial Process Safety

Chemical processing plants operate under highly dynamic conditions. Failures in monitoring critical variables such as temperature, pressure, and flow rate can result in severe operational and safety consequences.

## Machine Learning for Fault Detection

Machine learning techniques such as Random Forests, Isolation Forests, and LSTM networks have shown strong performance in identifying anomalies within industrial process data.

## Explainable AI

In safety-critical systems, transparency is essential. Explainable AI methods such as SHAP provide insight into model decisions and increase operator trust.

## Research Gap

Most existing studies focus on process optimization. Fewer studies investigate how AI can serve as a proactive safety layer that predicts hazards before conventional alarm systems are triggered.
