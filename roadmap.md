# Research Roadmap

## Phase 1: Literature Review
Review AI safety, anomaly detection, and industrial process monitoring research.

## Phase 2: Dataset Analysis
Analyze Tennessee Eastman Process and industrial sensor datasets.

## Phase 3: Baseline Monitoring
Implement traditional threshold-based monitoring.

## Phase 4: Machine Learning Models
Evaluate Random Forest, XGBoost, Isolation Forest, and LSTM models.

## Phase 5: Risk Scoring Framework
Develop a safety-oriented risk classification system.

## Phase 6: Explainable AI
Apply SHAP and feature importance techniques.

## Phase 7: Evaluation
Compare AI-based monitoring with conventional approaches.

## Phase 8: Future Extensions
Investigate digital twins, reinforcement learning, and autonomous safety monitoring systems.
